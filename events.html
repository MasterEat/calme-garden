A Sanctuary for Every Occasion

From intimate workshops and rustic retreats to vibrant bazaars and celebratory dinners, Calme Garden offers a versatile canvas tailored to your vision. Our spaces are designed to foster connection, creativity, and a deep sense of Mediterranean hospitality.

OUTDOOR

Herbal Garden | Βοτανικός Κήπος

An aromatic haven perfect for morning yoga, acoustic sessions, or botanical workshops surrounded by native Greek flora.

INDOOR

Main Building | Κεντρικό Κτίριο

A minimalist, light-filled space ideal for corporate seminars, exhibitions, or formal gatherings.

Horses Yard | Αυλή με τα Άλογα

Unique, spirited location for rustic festivals, markets, and photography sessions with our resident companions.

Amphitheater | Αμφιθέατρο

A dramatic, open-air stone theater designed for performances, talks, and sunset ceremonies overlooking the horizon.
