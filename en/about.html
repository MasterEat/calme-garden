Our Story

A meeting place for nature and culture.

Calme Garden

Calme Garden is not simply a farm. It is a living organism, an eco-cultural center born from the need to reconnect with the land and community. At the heart of our practice is regenerative agriculture, an approach that aims not only to preserve but to actively restore the ecosystem.

Through Evolving Cycles Organisation, we host international Erasmus+ programs and non-formal learning initiatives, offering a place where the theory of sustainability becomes lived experience. Here, the cycle of life—from seed to plate and from observation to action—is our compass.
