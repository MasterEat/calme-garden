At the heart of regeneration

Calme Garden is an eco-cultural center born from the need for connection. In a recovering landscape, we create a place where traditional knowledge meets contemporary sustainability. Here, every tree planted is a promise for the future and every workshop is a bond that strengthens our community.

From ashes to bloom

Our story is one of resilience. After devastating fires, Calme Garden became a symbol of hope. With the help of volunteers and the local community, we are bringing life back to the landscape, planting hundreds of trees and restoring the ecosystem step by step.

OUR JOURNEY

The garden is an open canvas. We host artists, educators and groups who share our vision for a slower, more meaningful way of life.
